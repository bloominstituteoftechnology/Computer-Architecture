### Bitwise Operators

What is the result of 11011111 NOR 00010111?

    11011111
|   00010111
------------
~   11011111
------------
    00100000

What is the result of 10101010 XOR 11110000?

    10101010
^   11110000
------------
    01011010

What is the result of 11011 AND 101?

    11011
&     101
---------
    00001
